About this area

Located in Anjuna, Fairfield by Marriott Goa Anjuna is in the entertainment district and near the beach. Chapora Fortress and Fort Aguada are notable landmarks, and the area's natural beauty can be seen at Baga Beach and Anjuna Beach. Splashdown Waterpark and Goa Science Centre are also worth visiting.
